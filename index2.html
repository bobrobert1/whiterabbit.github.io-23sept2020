white rabbit coffee co.
We serve Fernwood espresso and filter coffee, tea, muesli, toast by the slice, and other tasty snacks.

Whether you need a place to study, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a refill before hitting the trail, please drop by and enjoy. Come enjoy the coffee, savor the food, take in the art and good company.

White Rabbit Coffee Co. is a space for community and collaboration. We're not just coffee. We're a habitat of endless possibility.
